About VrittiAura

A Trusted Space for Clarity & Conscious Transformation

I'm Anita Mishra — known to clients across India and abroad as Asvita — an intuitive Vedic Tarot reader, numerologist and energy healer. VrittiAura is the home I've built for everyone who is ready to understand themselves more deeply and move forward with quiet confidence.

My work brings together Vedic Tarot, Numerology, Face Reading, Reiki, candle and crystal healing, manifestation coaching, and sacred rituals such as Lamba Fera and Humkara with Haleem. Every session is grounded in observation, intuition and time-honoured practice — never in fear or vague predictions.

People come to VrittiAura at turning points: career decisions, relationships, healing after loss, or simply a quiet pull to realign with their purpose. What they find here is a calm, judgement-free space where ancient wisdom meets practical, everyday guidance — so insight becomes something you can actually act on.

VrittiAura exists because I believe spiritual guidance should empower, not frighten. It should leave you feeling lighter, clearer and more in charge of your story — supported by remedies and rituals that are simple, ethical and meaningful.
